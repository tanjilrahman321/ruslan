About James

James M. Haddad is a principal at the Law Office of James M. Haddad, where his practice focuses on commercial litigation. His experience in the insurance industry makes him uniquely experienced and qualified to help his clients.

His clients value his unique and deep expertise, personal attention and responsiveness. As a sole practitioner, he can provide a continuity of care, cost-effective representation and flexible payment arrangements not often found at larger firms.

Mr. Haddad serves the legal needs of commercial clients, insurers, policy holders, construction companies, and others, in areas such as contract negotiation and drafting, intellectual property rights, complex insurance coverage issues, D&O, EPL, professional liability, and more. Mr. Haddad also represents individuals in personal injury and business matters.

Mr. Haddad is a graduate of Brown University (A.B., 1986) and Fordham University School of Law (J.D., 1993).
